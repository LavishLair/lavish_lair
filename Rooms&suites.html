Rooms & Suites

Modern and contemporary in design and ambiance, the suites and guestrooms offer city, lake and poolside views and are carefully designed to take the inconvenience out of business travel.

Delux Room

❮ ❯

Enjoy the relaxing 35 sq m contemporary room with a plush king bed, work area with enhanced lighting and high-speed Internet access. Maximum guests per room: 2 adults / 1 child

 

Premier Room

❮ ❯

Enjoy stunning views of river from a stylish 36-square-metre room offering plush king-size bed and work area with enhanced lighting. Maximum guests per room: 2 adults / 1 child

 

Delux Suite

❮ ❯

Magnificent city views welcome you to a 73-square-metre suite offering spacious living area, Shower and bathtub cubicle, king-size bed and two-way airport transfers. Maximum guests per room: 3 This is a standard suite.

 

Premier Suite

❮ ❯

A sophisticated 80 sq m suite with king bed, separate living area, walk-in wardrobe, and deluxe bathroom with soaking tub, rain shower, double vanity and glass shower cubical. The suite features contemporary interiors with beautiful artefacts, designer furniture, and interiors in pastel shades. Maximum guests per room: 3 This is a standard suite.
